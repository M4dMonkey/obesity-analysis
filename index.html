Prevalence of obesity

What is Obesity? It is defined as abnormal or excessive fat accumulation that may impair health. BMI, shorted for body mass index , means the weight in kilograms divided by the square of the height in meters . BMI is universally used as an index to classify overweight and obesity in adults. In this visualization, data used for obesity is based on BMI equal to or more than 30, which is defined by World Health Organization.
